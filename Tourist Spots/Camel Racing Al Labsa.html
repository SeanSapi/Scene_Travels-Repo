About Camel Racing at Al Labsa


Head to Al Labsa's camel racing track in Umm Al-Quwain for a day at the races (29 kilometres southeast of Umm Al-Quwain city). Throughout the cooler months (typically October to April), races are held on Fridays at around 7 a.m. Robot jockeys ride the camels, giving the old activity a modern, high-tech twist. Camel racing is as old as history itself. On the Arabian Peninsula, the native habitat of the dromedary can be traced to at least the early Islamic period, in the 7th-century CE. Labs is a village inland of the Emirate of Umm Al Quwain, United Arab Emirates. It is the location of the Al Labsah Camel Racing Track
