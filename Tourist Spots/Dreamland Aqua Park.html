Dreamland Aqua Park

About Dreamland Aqua Park


Since 1997

This massive amusement complex, which contains over 25 attractions, is a major hit with families. Aquatic themed rides, children's wading pools, twisters, a crazy river and kamikaze ride, and a large wave pool are among the numerous things to do here.

Check out their official website

Activities


TWISTER

Slide inside a 40 meter tunnel long, Spin yourself fast on the bowl’s inner walls, And then take a 2-meter nose dive! It is F.A.N.T.A.S.T.I.C.


FAMILY RAFT RIDE

The tube accommodates 5 people and will take you on a rough ride. You will scream your hearts out on this adrenalin-pumping raft ride.


LAKE-O-WAVES

No tsunami in Dreamland. This gigantic wave pool is the largest in the region.


DEAD SEA

Live the Dead Sea experience in our high salinity pool. It’s one of a kind.
