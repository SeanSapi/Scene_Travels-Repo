Dhayah Fort

About


The Dhayah Fort played an essential part in the development of Ras Al-Khaimah as a defensive military structure. To the south of the hill, there are other ruined defenses and guard posts. Much of the fortifications here were destroyed in 1891 during a conflict between the emirate and British soldiers.

Activities


It’s easily the fortress with the most stunning view in the UAE. With mountains to the east and palm oases disappearing into the sea on the west, it’s a great viewing spot for both sunset and sunrise.


With kid-friendly steps leading to the top directly from a decent sized parking lot, it ticks many family-friendly boxes.


If you’re polite enough with the staff at the guest house, they’ll probably let you use the toilet there too.
