Mall Of The Emirates

About


The project was launched in October 2003 at an estimated cost of AED 800 million (US$218 million) and was scheduled to be completed in September 2005. The architecture in the three-story complex combines Arabic and Mediterranean elements, with each level connected to a car park. The mall was designed by the American architectural firm F+A Architects. The main building contract was handled by Khansaheb, while the Ski Dubai contract was given to Pomagalski. The chair lifts in the snow park and the structural steelworks were handled by Emirates Building System and the piling by Bauer. The mall opened for business on 28 September 2005 with an official inauguration in November.

Activities


Ziplining is thrilling enough, but have you ever done it surrounded by snow and ice? Try Ski Dubai’s new Snow Bullet ride, where you fly down the custom-made 150m long zip line and above the slopes and snow park. You’ll be soaring 16m above the ground, so we think it’s definitely one of the coolest things to do at Mall of the Emirates.


Next on our list of things to do in Mall of the Emirates is the towering climbing wall at the Go Sports megastore. It’s great for adults and kids, and with all equipment provided, it’s one of the best ways to get fit and active, especially in a shopping mall!


You may think meeting penguins in a desert is unlikely, but Dubai will surprise you! Now, meet the beautiful Gentoo and King Penguins, who have the cutest names like Bubbles, Cuddly and Pumpkin! One of Mall of the Emirates’ highlights, it’s your chance to get up close with these stunning, waddling creatures that call Ski Dubai their home!


Here’s your chance to learn to ski or snowboard in the world-class destination of Ski Dubai! Their Ski School offers lessons for all skill levels – for beginners who have never touched a ski pole or seasoned snowboarders who fly down the slopes. The Ski School in Mall of the Emirates has ski professionals as your instructors, so you’ll be up and skiing in no time! There are plenty of packages available, including group and private lessons, so you can tailor your lessons to suit you!


Challenge your sharp-shooting skills at this one-of-a-kind adventure experience! Regarded as one of the best activities at Mall of the Emirates, Astro Blasters is a thrilling gaming experience set in space! Pick up your Nerf gun and battle to the top position to earn your title as an Astro Blasters Commander.


Wondering what to do at Mall of the Emirates? Tucked away in Magic Planet is Gravity Trampoline Park, one of the best attractions at Mall of the Emirates. Ideal for kids and adults, test your leaping skills and bounce around at this jump place in Dubai. It’s perfect for the kids, while the adults sneak away for some shopping.


This may just be one of the most luxurious cinema experiences’ in Dubai! VOX Cinemas has partnered with Michelin-starred Chef Gary Rhodes to bring you an exclusive dinner and movie experience. Instead of popcorn buckets, you’ll be dining on afternoon tea, mezze platters, gourmet mains, and decadent desserts. Think comfy blankets, pillows, footrests, and in-cinema waiter service! If you’re looking for things to do at Mall of the Emirates, indulge yourself with Theatre by Rhodes at VOX Cinemas.


One of the latest attractions at Mall of the Emirates Dubai is the brand-new Magnum Store that has opened in front of VOX Cinemas, the first in the UAE! Create your own personalized Magnum ice cream bar by choosing your ice cream and chocolate coating (dark, milk, or white chocolate). Finish off your creation with three toppings from an interesting selection, that has options like raspberry crunch, chocolate blossoms, and even chili flakes!
