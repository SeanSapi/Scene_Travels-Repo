Yas WaterWorld

About Yas Waterworld


Since 2013

Yas Waterworld is a massive amusement park with 40 rides, including many waterslides and an inner-tube river ride that spans 37 acres. There's also the world's first hydromagnetic tornado water rafting attraction, the world's biggest surfable sheet wave, and the Jebel Drop, a free-fall speed waterslide for serious thrill junkies. Yas Waterworld’s theme is based on an original story developed exclusively for the waterpark: The legend of The Lost Pearl. This tale is inspired by Emirati culture and history and follows the adventures of Dana, a young Emirati girl in search of a legendary pearl that had once brought prosperity to her village.

Activities


Cinesplash (family rides)

Don’t miss this world class attraction, live the legend in 5D! will take you to new depths as the entire cinema floods with water up to your knees.


Yehal (kids rides)

Little water lovers can splash in their own paddling pool, take on three tots slides, and get soaked by a dumping bucket!


Rush Rider (thrill rides)

Ease into bodyboarding, until you flow like a pro. Take on the endless waves until you graduate to Bubbles Barrel!


Jebel Drop (also thrill rides)

Slide from the height of a mountaintop on the region's tallest speed slide. Start from the highest point and plummet from a 10 stories slide.
