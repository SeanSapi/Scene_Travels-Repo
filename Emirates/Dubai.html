DUBAI FRAME

-Timings-
Saturday - Friday: 9 AM - 9 PM

Dubai Frame, the UAE's newest cultural monument, is an iconic building that 'frames' breathtaking vistas of Old and New Dubai while also serving as a symbolic bridge uniting the emirate's rich history with its spectacular present. Dubai Frame, which offers breathtaking panoramic views of the city, commemorates the narrative of Dubai, from its early founding to its ambitious aspirations for future expansion.

WHAT IS DUBAI?

Dubai, the glitzy capital of the United Arab Emirates, is a popular tourist destination. This metropolis of high-rises and shopping malls has evolved from a desert outpost to a popular tourist destination, with visitors flocking for bargains, sunlight, and family fun.
