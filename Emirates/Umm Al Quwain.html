Camel Racing at Al Labsa

-Timings-
Open from 7 AM - 9 A

Head to Al Labsa's camel racing track in Umm Al-Quwain for a day at the races (29 kilometres southeast of Umm Al-Quwain city). Throughout the cooler months (typically October to April), races are held on Fridays at around 7 a.m. Robot jockeys ride the camels, giving the old activity a modern, high-tech twist.

This 250-year-old landmark was previously the Royal family's residence, known as the Umm Al Quwain Fort at the time. It is now the UAE's official museum. This museum displays the history of the UAE in one spectacular castle, with visitors ranging from GCC expats to school field excursions.

WHAT IS UMM AL QUWAIN?

Umm Al Quwain is the capital and largest city of the Emirate of Umm Al Quwain in the United Arab Emirates. The city is located on the peninsula of Khor Al Bidiyah, with the nearest major cities being Sharjah to the southwest and Ras Al Khaimah to the northeast.
