AL BIDYA MOSQUE

-Timings-
Whole Week: 08:00 am to 06:30 pm

The mud-brick Al-Bidyah Mosque, located 35 kilometres north of Fujairah city, is the UAE's oldest mosque and was named after the community that once surrounded it. Excavation of the area surrounding the mosque revealed that it had been inhabited for 4,000 years. A gigantic Iron Age tomb was uncovered in a big Islamic graveyard just to the north, revealing remnants of pottery, metal arrowheads, and other items going back to at least 1000 BC.

FUJAIRAH FORT

-Timings-
Whole Week: 08:00 am to 05:00 pm

Fujairah Fort is a fort in the city of Fujairah, United Arab Emirates (UAE). Dating back to the 16th century, it is among the oldest as well as the largest castles in the country. It is noted for playing significant role in fighting back the wave of colonialism. Today, it is among the main tourist attractions in the city. It is probably the oldest fort in the UAE and was occupied by the Wahhabists

WHAT IS FUJAIRAH?

Fujairah in the United Arab EmirateS is the seventh-largest city, located on the Gulf of Oman. It is the only Emirati city on the UAE's east coast. Fujairah is known for its beaches and the solitude and relaxation they present. Offshore activities include scuba diving and snorkelling and there are many beautiful sites for these available in the area.
